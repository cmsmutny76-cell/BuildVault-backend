## 🔄 Convert SVG to PNG (Required for Play Store)

### **Method 1: Online Converter (Easiest - 2 minutes)**

1. **Go to:** https://svgtopng.com or https://cloudconvert.com/svg-to-png

2. **Upload file:** `buildvault-icon-simple.svg`

3. **Set dimensions:**
   - Width: 512px
   - Height: 512px
   - ✅ Check "Maintain aspect ratio"

4. **Download** as PNG

5. **Rename** to: `app-icon-512.png`

6. **Repeat for other sizes** if needed:
   - 1024 x 1024 (optional high-res)
   - 192 x 192 (adaptive icon)

### **Method 2: Use Figma (Professional - 5 minutes)**

1. **Go to:** https://www.figma.com (free account)

2. **Create new file** → Import SVG

3. **Right-click** on imported logo → "Export"

4. **Set export settings:**
   - Format: PNG
   - Size: 512w x 512h
   - Scale: 1x (or 2x for @2x)

5. **Export** and download

### **Method 3: Inkscape (Desktop App - 10 minutes)**

1. **Download:** https://inkscape.org (free, open-source)

2. **Open** `buildvault-icon-simple.svg` in Inkscape

3. **File** → Export PNG Image

4. **Set:**
   - Width: 512 px
   - Height: 512 px
   - DPI: 96

5. **Export** button

6. **Save as:** `app-icon-512.png`

## 🎨 Logo Usage Guidelines

### **Do's:**
- ✅ Use on orange, blue, or white backgrounds
- ✅ Maintain clear spacing around logo
- ✅ Keep original aspect ratio
- ✅ Use PNG for digital, SVG for print

### **Don'ts:**
- ❌ Don't stretch or distort
- ❌ Don't change colors (unless one-color version needed)
- ❌ Don't add effects (shadows, glows) to the logo itself
- ❌ Don't use on busy backgrounds that obscure logo

### **Minimum Sizes:**
- Digital: 32px (favicon)
- Print: 0.5 inches
- App icon: 48px (Android)
