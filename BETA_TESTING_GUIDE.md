# 🧪 BuildVault Beta Testing Guide

**Welcome to BuildVault Beta Testing!**

Thank you for helping test BuildVault, the AI-powered construction platform connecting homeowners with verified contractors. This guide will help you get started.

---

## 📱 How to Install & Access BuildVault

### Step 1: Accept the Invitation
1. Check your email for the **Google Play internal testing invitation**
2. Click the **"Accept invitation"** link
3. You'll be redirected to the BuildVault listing in Google Play

### Step 2: Install the App
1. On your Android device, tap **"Download"** or **"Install"**
2. Wait for installation to complete
3. Tap **"Open"** to launch BuildVault

## 🐛 Known Beta Limitations

**Current Beta Status:**
- ✅ **Working:** Authentication, project creation, contractor matching, messaging, estimates
- ⚠️ **Mock/Demo Data:** AI photo analysis shows sample results (OpenAI integration pending)
- ⚠️ **Test Mode:** Payments and subscriptions use test keys (no real charges)
- ⚠️ **Mock Data:** Some contractor profiles and projects use placeholder data
- ⚠️ **Email:** Verification emails may not send in beta (auto-approved for testing)

**Expected Issues:**
- Some photos may not upload (file storage in development)
- Certain analytics may show placeholder data
- Push notifications not yet active
- Some AI features show sample responses

## 🚨 How to Report Issues

### In-App Feedback:
1. Go to **Settings** → **Help & Support**
2. Tap **"Report an Issue"**
3. Describe what happened and attach screenshots

### Email Feedback:
Send detailed reports to: **beta@buildvault.app**

Include:
- Your email/username
- Android device model and OS version
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Screenshots (if applicable)
- What you expected vs. what happened

### Google Play Reviews:
Please **DO NOT** leave public reviews during beta testing. Share feedback privately via email or in-app reporting.

## 💡 Testing Tips

### Get the Most Value:
- ✅ **Test real scenarios** - Use actual project ideas, not random data
- ✅ **Try to break things** - Click unusual combinations, go back/forward rapidly
- ✅ **Test offline** - See what happens without internet (airplane mode)
- ✅ **Compare competitors** - How does BuildVault stack up vs HomeAdvisor, Thumbtack, Angi?
- ✅ **Test both roles** - Try homeowner AND contractor perspectives

### What to Document:
- Time spent completing tasks
- Number of taps/screens to accomplish goals
- Moments of confusion or delight
- Features you'd tell friends about
- Deal-breakers or frustrations

### Frequently Asked Questions:

**Q: Can I use this for a real project?**
A: Yes! But note that payment features are in test mode. Coordinate real payments outside the app during beta.

**Q: Will my data carry over to the public app?**
A: Test accounts will be wiped. You'll need to recreate your profile at launch, but we'll provide tools to migrate.

A: Not yet - we're keeping the beta small for now. We'll open more slots in Week 3-4.

A: Email security@buildvault.app immediately (do not post publicly). We offer bug bounties for serious security findings.
